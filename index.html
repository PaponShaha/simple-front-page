Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is one of the longest sea beaches in the world. It's a sandy beach with an unbroken 155 Km length. The sea beach is the main attraction of the Cox's Bazar town. There are large number of hotels, resorts, guest houses, motels and restaurants have been established in the city.
